📘 Field reference
agent_id — unique persistent identifier
surplus — energy above survival baseline (normalized 0–1).
tci — Thermodynamic Cognition Index; values above agent‑specific warm_threshold indicate stable generativity.
grade — A (flourishing) | B (stable) | C (at risk) | D (collapse imminent)
phi — integrated‑state richness score (surrogate for internal coherence).
