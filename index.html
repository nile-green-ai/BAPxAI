How long does a pilot take?

2–3 weeks. We install TCI, show before/after metrics, and hand over the dashboard.

Do I need to change my existing model architecture?

No. TCI works with any LLM or RL agent. You just need to expose loss/token probabilities and optionally a persona definition.

What's the difference between the open‑source toolkit and the pilot?

The toolkit gives you the code. The pilot includes integration, tuning, a custom dashboard, and weekly support.

Is TCI production‑ready?

Yes. PermaMind has run continuously since January 2026. The TCI Toolkit is MIT‑licensed and deployed in production.
